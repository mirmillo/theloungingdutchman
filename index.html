On Tap

Calder Saison

Brewed at the 2016 Big Brew Day at Calder Plaza with Tony B, Rob F and Matt C. All Michigan ingredients. Jarrylo hops, grains from Pilot Malt House in Byron Center. Eagle River Ale Yeast is the second in a series of indigenous Michigan strains. It was isolated from the shore of Lake Superior near Eagle River Michigan. This yeast will produce excellent Belgian style ales. This yeast is rich in both spice and phenols exhibits predominantly fruity esters, similar to a Belgian ale strain.

5.5% ABV

Multitasker Cream Ale

An ale version of the light, fizzy American lager style, cream ale is a specialty of the eastern US. Cream Ale is medium-light bodied and smooth, gold in color and low in bitterness; the specialty grain blend adds some complexity with a clean, sweet malt profile and a hint of buttered toast in the aroma and flavor. Followed Northern Brewer's recipe. Cleaning kegs, keezer and preparing to keg beer at the same time, for future reference I should not be allowed to multitask while brewing - missed temps and volumes like crazy

5.5% ABV

Simpatico SMaSH London

A single malt and single hop ale. Maris Otter, an English 2-row barley known for it's golden colour and uniquely rish and slightly nutty flavor. Combined with the complex Simcoe hops mixing fruity and piney aromas. London ESB yeast finishes this style.

7.0% ABV

Yellow Dog Pale Redux

All grain version of the first brew I ever made over seven years ago. Aniticipation and expectations are high. White wheat and Chocolate malt blend with Nugget and Perle hops create and American Pale Ale.

4.6% ABV
